## Step 2: Network Mapping Strategy

### The Networking Pyramid

```
                    ┌─────────────┐
                    │   Mentors   │  ← 2-3 people
                    ├─────────────┤
                    │  Champions  │  ← 5-10 people
                    ├─────────────┤
                    │  Connectors │  ← 15-20 people
                    ├─────────────┤
                    │  Allies     │  ← 30-50 people
                    ├─────────────┤
                    │  Contacts   │  ← 100+ people
                    └─────────────┘
```

### Relationship Types

| Type | Purpose | Investment |
|------|---------|------------|
| **Mentors** | Career guidance, wisdom | High (monthly check-ins) |
| **Champions** | Advocate for you, make introductions | High (quarterly touch) |
| **Connectors** | Introduce you to others | Medium (regular updates) |
| **Allies** | Share opportunities, information | Medium (occasional check-ins) |
| **Contacts** | Broad network, surface-level | Low (annual touch) |

## Step 4: LinkedIn Networking Strategy

### Profile Optimization
- Professional headshot
- Compelling headline (not just job title)
- Detailed summary with keywords
- Experience with metrics
- Skills endorsements
- Recommendations

### Content Strategy
- Share industry insights (2-3x/week)
- Comment on others' posts (daily)
- Write articles (monthly)
- Share achievements (quarterly)

### Outreach Templates

**Cold Connection Request:**

```
Hi [Name],

I came across your profile and was impressed by your work at [Company] in [specific area]. I'm a [your role] with experience in [your skills], and I'm particularly interested in [their work/project].

I'd love to connect and learn more about your experience at [Company]. No strings attached—just genuinely interested in your perspective.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Follow-up After Connection:**

```
Hi [Name],

Thanks for connecting! I've been following [Company]'s work in [area] and had a few questions I was hoping you might be able to help with:

1. [Specific question about their work]
2. [Question about company culture]
3. [Question about industry trends]

Would you have 15-20 minutes for a virtual coffee chat? I'd really appreciate your insights.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Informational Interview Request:**

```
Hi [Name],

I'm a [your role] exploring opportunities in [industry/field]. I've been following your career path from [Company A] to [Company B], and I'm really impressed by [specific achievement].

I'm considering a similar career move and would love to learn from your experience. Would you have 20-30 minutes for a virtual coffee chat? I'd be happy to work around your schedule.

Specifically, I'd love to hear about:
- How you transitioned from [Company A] to [Company B]
- What skills were most valuable in that transition
- Any advice for someone looking to make a similar move

Thank you for considering!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

## Step 5: Event Networking Strategy

### Before the Event
- Research attendees and speakers
- Set specific goals (e.g., "meet 5 people in product")
- Prepare elevator pitch
- Bring business cards (or have LinkedIn QR code ready)

### During the Event
- Arrive early (less overwhelming)
- Ask open-ended questions
- Listen more than you talk
- Take notes on people you meet
- Follow up in real-time (connect on LinkedIn)

### After the Event
- Send follow-up emails within 24 hours
- Connect on LinkedIn with personalized note
- Add to your CRM or contact list
- Schedule follow-up coffee chats

### Conversation Starters

- "What brings you to this event?"
- "What's the most interesting thing you're working on right now?"
- "How do you see [industry trend] affecting your work?"
- "What's your biggest challenge right now?"
